# CRAFT — Non-fiction writing craft

Principles and techniques injected into every drafting prompt beyond style-specific rules.
To be developed in detail during v0.1 pipeline implementation.

## Argumentation
- Every section advances a single claim
- Claims stack — each builds on the previous
- Counterarguments are engaged, not dismissed
- Evidence types: empirical, logical, anecdotal, authoritative — match to style profile

## Structure
- The lede earns the reader's next 30 seconds
- Sections follow a predictable shape: claim → evidence → analysis → transition
- Paragraphs are atomic — one idea each

## Evidence
- Primary sources > secondary > tertiary
- Quote sparingly — paraphrase when the idea matters, quote when the words matter
- Dates matter — 2023 data isn't "recent" in 2026
